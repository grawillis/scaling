Phase 2: $1M–$3M

Systematization

Move from founder-dependent to system-dependent

Primary Goal

Build systematic processes that reduce founder dependency and create predictable, scalable operations. This phase transforms your business from a founder-led operation to a system-driven organization.

Define Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

Create detailed criteria for your ideal customers based on seats, industries, compliance needs, budget, and technology profile.

Company size (seat count ranges)
Target industries and verticals
Compliance requirements (HIPAA, SOX, etc.)
Technology stack and maturity
Budget range and decision-making process

Productize Services

Transform custom services into standardized packages with clear deliverables, tiered pricing, and defined SLAs.

Good/Better/Best service tiers
Standardized deliverables per tier
Clear SLA definitions and penalties
Per user/device minimums and rate cards

Repeatable Sales Process

Install a structured sales process in your CRM with clear stages, exit criteria, and automated follow-ups.

CRM pipeline with defined stages
Exit criteria for each stage
Automated follow-up sequences
Proposal templates and MSAs

Launch Account Management

Implement quarterly Technology Business Reviews (TBRs) to drive projects, expansion, and strengthen client relationships.

TBR agenda and process templates
Account manager role definition
Expansion opportunity identification
Project pipeline management

Prune Non-ICP Clients

Systematically review and either upgrade or exit clients that don't fit your ICP to raise average MRR and improve focus.

Audit Current Clients

Review all clients against ICP criteria

Upgrade Opportunities

Move good clients to higher-tier packages

Graceful Exits

Professional offboarding for poor fits

Leadership Cadence

Establish regular leadership rhythms to ensure consistent execution and continuous improvement.

Weekly Operations

  • • Pipeline review and forecast
  • • Service delivery metrics
  • • Resource allocation
  • • Issue escalation and resolution

Monthly Metrics

  • • Financial performance review
  • • KPI dashboard analysis
  • • Client satisfaction scores
  • • Team performance metrics

Quarterly Planning

  • • Strategic initiative review
  • • Market analysis and positioning
  • • Resource planning and hiring
  • • Goal setting and target updates
