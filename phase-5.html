Scaling for Exit

Build a business that works without you

Non-Negotiable Exit Criteria

Recurring growth margin covers OpEx and fair salaries
Pre-tax net profit ≥ 10% after founder salary
15%+ year-over-year growth
No single client > 10% of revenue

Strategic Framework

Set Your BHAG & Financial Freedom Number

Annual income × 25 ÷ 0.76

Example Calculation:

If you want $500K/year post-exit: $500K × 25 ÷ 0.76 = $16.4M exit
