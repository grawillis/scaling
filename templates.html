Service Agreement Essentials

Required Elements

  • Service scope and deliverables
  • Exclusions and limitations
  • SLA definitions and penalties
  • Change control process
  • Auto-renewal clauses
  • CPI increases (3-5% annually)

Pro Tip

Include a "service credit" system instead of financial penalties for SLA breaches. This protects margins while maintaining accountability.

// Service Agreement Template Structure
SERVICE AGREEMENT
// Parties and Definitions
Provider: [Your MSP Name]
Client: [Client Name]
Term: [Start Date] to [End Date]
// Service Scope
Included Services:
• 24/7 monitoring and alerting
• Patch management
• Backup monitoring
• Help desk support
// SLA Targets
Response Time: 15 minutes
Resolution Time: 4 hours
Uptime: 99.9%
