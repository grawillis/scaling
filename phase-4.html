Valley of Death Navigation

Most MSPs plateau or decline 12-24 months after $5M due to infrastructure, finance, and team gaps

The $5M Reality

The $5M mark is where most MSPs hit a wall. Systems that worked at $3M break down. Cash flow gets tight. Quality suffers. The founder gets pulled back into daily operations. This phase requires immediate triage and systematic stabilization.

First 30-Day Triage

Finance

Emergency billing audit
Tighten payment terms immediately
AR automation go-live
Price reset on underpriced services

Capacity

Headcount plan tied to pipeline
Freeze non-critical hires
Utilization analysis and optimization
Resource reallocation

Quality

Implement quality gates
Problem management process
Post-incident reviews
Enforce ICP strictly
