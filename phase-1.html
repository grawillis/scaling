Phase 1: Pre-$1M

Foundation Building

Make growth repeatable without chaos

Primary Goal

Establish the foundational systems and processes that make growth repeatable and scalable. This phase is about moving from reactive chaos to proactive, systematized operations.

Define Services

Create clear service definitions with scope, exclusions, SLAs, pricing, and profit per service.

Document service scope and deliverables
Define exclusions and change control process
Set SLA targets and penalties
Calculate profit margin per service

Document SOPs

Create standard operating procedures for your most common tickets, onboarding, patching, backups, and escalation paths.

Top 10 ticket types documented
Client onboarding checklist
Patching and maintenance procedures
Backup and recovery processes

Track Profit per Client

Implement tracking to understand which clients are profitable and which are draining resources.

Time tracking by client and service
Monthly profit analysis per client
Identify and reprice low-margin accounts
Consider exiting unprofitable clients

Calculate CAC

Understand your true customer acquisition cost and benchmark against industry standards.

Track all sales and marketing expenses
Calculate CAC per new client
Benchmark: SMB ~$585, Mid-market ~$4,438
Target: CAC within 1-1.5x benchmark

Common Pitfalls to Avoid

Selling to Everyone

Not all clients are good clients. Focus on your ideal customer profile.

Underpricing Labor-Heavy Work

Price services based on actual time and resource consumption.

No AR Controls

Poor accounts receivable management leads to high DSO and cash flow issues.
